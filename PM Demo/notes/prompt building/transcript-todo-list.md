## Notes for Background Agents

1. Each transcript should be exactly 4,000 words
2. Include realistic dialogue with natural conversation flow
3. Reference specific recruiting metrics where appropriate (time-to-fill, sourcing channel effectiveness, etc.)
4. Include at least 2-3 specific examples of roles they've recently recruited for
5. Make the pain points feel genuine and specific, not generic
6. Include realistic budget discussions and procurement considerations
7. Reference competitor tools naturally in conversation
8. Show variety in how the filter/persona pain point manifests - don't make them all sound the same
9. Include realistic recruiter names and backgrounds when they share anecdotes
10. Balance positive feedback with constructive criticism to feel authentic
