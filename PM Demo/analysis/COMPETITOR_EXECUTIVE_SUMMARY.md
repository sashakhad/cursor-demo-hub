## Key Market Insights

### 1. Persona-Based Sourcing is the Killer Use Case
**Every company interviewed** needs to differentiate between role types that appear similar:
- Infrastructure engineers: distributed systems vs. database kernel vs. platform
- Backend engineers: fintech vs. payments vs. risk management vs. fraud
- ML roles: researchers vs. data scientists vs. ML platform engineers

**Winner**: Tool that best supports complex, reusable, shareable persona/filter combinations

### 2. Data Quality is Make-or-Break
Greenhouse AI's 33% bounce rate makes it unusable despite being "free." Companies will pay for tools with better data (Jewel 12%, LinkedIn 8%).

### 3. "Black Box AI" is Rejected
Recruiters want transparency and control. Quotes:
- *"I don't want that. I want tools that make me more efficient, not tools that try to replace my judgment."*
- *"I need to understand why I'm seeing certain candidates. The black box AI thing doesn't work for me."*

### 4. Integration Matters More Than Features
Tool sprawl is real. Ops teams spend 5-10 hours/week just pulling reports from multiple systems.

### 5. Saved Searches are Most Valuable Feature
Power users have 12-15+ saved searches. This feature alone drives switching decisions.

## Top 3 Product Opportunities

### 1. üöÄ Shared Persona Library (HIGH IMPACT)
**Problem**: Saved searches are individual, take months to build  
**Solution**: Team library with templates, sharing, and customization  
**Evidence**: Mentioned by 6+ companies as top need  
**Impact**: Accelerates onboarding from 2 months to 2 weeks, captures institutional knowledge

### 2. üöÄ Fresher Data (HIGH IMPACT)
**Problem**: 20-30% of data is stale (quarterly updates insufficient)  
**Solution**: Monthly or real-time updates, freshness indicators  
**Evidence**: Universal pain point across all transcripts  
**Impact**: Major differentiator vs. ALL competitors

### 3. üöÄ Candidate Ranking/Prioritization (HIGH IMPACT)
**Problem**: Good filters still produce 200+ candidates - which to contact first?  
**Solution**: AI-powered ranking with transparent reasoning  
**Evidence**: Mentioned by 4+ companies  
**Impact**: Reduces manual review time by 50%+

## Competitive Threats & Responses

### Threat 1: Greenhouse improves AI sourcing data quality
**Likelihood**: Medium (they're aware of the problem)  
**Response**: Maintain data quality lead, emphasize saved searches advantage  
**Moat**: Power users on Jewel have built 12-15+ personas - switching cost is high

### Threat 2: Juicebox raises funding, improves enterprise features
**Likelihood**: High (they're gaining traction)  
**Response**: Move upmarket faster, lock in enterprise customers with long contracts  
**Moat**: Enterprise readiness (SOC 2, security) takes 18+ months to build

### Threat 3: LinkedIn builds better saved search functionality
**Likelihood**: Low (their focus is on broad market, not niche recruiting)  
**Response**: Continue specializing in technical recruiting personas  
**Moat**: Deep expertise in technical recruiting workflows

### Threat 4: New AI-native competitor emerges
**Likelihood**: Medium (lots of AI excitement, funding available)  
**Response**: Invest in AI features but maintain transparency/control  
**Moat**: Existing customer relationships and data on what actually works

3. **Expansion**: Convert existing ATS customers to AI sourcing users
