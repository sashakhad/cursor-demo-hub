## üìä Key Findings at a Glance

### Market Structure
- **Universal baseline:** LinkedIn Recruiter (100% adoption, but insufficient)
- **Primary platform:** Jewel (strong with power users)
- **Failed bundled:** Greenhouse AI (33% email bounce rate kills adoption)
- **Emerging challenger:** Juicebox (budget-friendly alternative)
- **Niche players:** Gem (CRM), SeekOut (large DB), HireEZ (AI features)

### Jewel's Position
**Strengths:**
- Saved searches ("game changer")
- AI intake form (major differentiator)
- Data quality (12% bounce vs. 33% for Greenhouse)
- Clean UX ("refreshing")
- Strong analytics

**Threats:**
- Budget pressure vs. "free" Greenhouse
- Juicebox emergence (cheaper, good enough)
- Can't share saved searches across teams
- 1-2 month learning curve

### Top 3 Product Opportunities
1. **Shared persona library** (HIGH IMPACT) - Most requested
2. **Fresher data** (HIGH IMPACT) - Universal pain point
3. **Candidate ranking** (HIGH IMPACT) - 200+ candidates, which to contact?

## üìà Market Insights

### Critical Trends
1. **Persona-based sourcing** is the killer use case (every company needs this)
2. **Data quality** is make-or-break (33% bounce = unusable)
3. **"Black box AI"** is rejected (recruiters want control + transparency)
4. **Integration** matters more than features (tool sprawl is real)
5. **Saved searches** are most valuable feature (drives switching decisions)

### Customer Segments
- **Hot prospects:** Goldman Sachs, Snowflake (evaluating)
- **Strong users:** Stripe, Databricks, Netflix, Uber
- **At risk:** Any customer with <50% license utilization
- **Lost/never won:** Figma (using Juicebox)
